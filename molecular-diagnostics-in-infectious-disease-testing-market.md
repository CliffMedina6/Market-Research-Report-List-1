Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) offers quicker and more accurate results than PCR. Microarrays allow simultaneous testing of multiple targets. Other techniques in this market include enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and mass spectrometry.</p></p>
