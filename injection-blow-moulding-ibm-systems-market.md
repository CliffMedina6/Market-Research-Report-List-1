<p><p>Injection-Blow Moulding (IBM) Systems are advanced manufacturing systems used to produce hollow plastic products, such as bottles, containers, and other packaging solutions. This process integrates two primary steps: Injection Moulding and Blow Moulding. First, a preform is created through injection moulding, where melted plastic is injected into a mold to form a parison. Then, the parison is transferred to a blow moulding station, where it is inflated with compressed air to take the shape of the desired product.</p><p>The Injection-Blow Moulding (IBM) Systems Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
