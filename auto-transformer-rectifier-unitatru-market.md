<p><p>An Auto Transformer Rectifier Unit (ATRU) is a device used in high voltage direct current (HVDC) transmission systems to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). There are three types of ATRU markets: 6 pulse, 12 pulse, and 18 pulse. These types refer to the number of pulses generated during the conversion process. The higher the number of pulses, the smoother the DC output waveform, resulting in reduced harmonics and improved power quality. This is achieved through the use of additional transformers and rectifiers in the ATRU design.</p></p>
