<p><p>Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) Systems are used for capturing and reading vehicle registration plates. There are two types of ANPR systems: stationary and portable. Stationary ANPR systems are permanently installed at fixed locations, such as toll booths or parking lots, to continuously monitor the passing vehicles. Portable ANPR systems, on the other hand, can be easily transported and set up at temporary locations, such as police roadblocks or mobile speed cameras, to capture and analyze the vehicle registration plates. Both types of ANPR systems play a crucial role in enhancing security, traffic management, and law enforcement.</p></p>
