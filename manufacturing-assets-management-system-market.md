<p><p>Manufacturing Assets Management Systems (MAMS) include various market types such as Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Real-Time Location System (RTLS), Barcode, and Global Positioning System (GPS). RFID technology allows manufacturers to track and manage assets using radio waves, improving efficiency and reducing human effort. RTLS uses advanced sensors and software to provide real-time visibility and tracking of assets within a facility. Barcode systems use printed codes for asset identification and tracking. GPS technology enables manufacturers to accurately track assets across different locations using satellite signals. All these systems enhance asset management processes and aid in optimizing manufacturing operations.</p></p>
