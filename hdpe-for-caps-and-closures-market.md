<p><p>The HDPE for Caps and Closures market comprises two types: Injection Molding and Compression Molding. Injection Molding involves melting HDPE material and injecting it into a mold to create caps and closures. This method is widely used due to its high production volume and cost-effectiveness. Compression Molding involves directly compressing HDPE material into a mold, resulting in caps and closures with excellent structural integrity. This method is suitable for producing intricate designs and is often preferred for smaller production runs or specialty applications.</p></p>
