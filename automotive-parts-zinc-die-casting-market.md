<p><p>The automotive parts zinc die casting market comprises various types, including pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, semi-solid die casting, and others. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten zinc under high pressure into a die cavity to create the desired shape. Vacuum die casting uses a vacuum to remove air from the die cavity during the process. Squeeze die casting employs a combination of pressure and slow injection to achieve intricate designs. Semi-solid die casting uses partially solidified zinc to enhance the part's mechanical properties. Other techniques involve unique variations in die casting processes.</p></p>
