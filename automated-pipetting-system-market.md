<p><p>The automated pipetting system market includes two primary types: standalone benchtop workstations and multi-instrument systems. Standalone benchtop workstations are compact and designed for individual use, providing automated pipetting functionalities for various applications. On the other hand, multi-instrument systems are larger and offer automated pipetting functions alongside additional features like plate handling, shaking, and temperature control. These systems are suitable for high-throughput laboratories requiring multitasking capabilities. Both types cater to the need for precise and efficient pipetting processes, with the multi-instrument system offering more advanced functionalities.</p></p>
