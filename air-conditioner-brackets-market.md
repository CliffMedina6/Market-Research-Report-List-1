<p><p>The air conditioner brackets market is divided into four types: ordinary angle bracket, steel plate bracket, galvanized steel bracket, and others. Ordinary angle brackets are made of regular materials and provide basic support for air conditioners. Steel plate brackets offer more durability and strength due to their construction with steel plates. Galvanized steel brackets are coated with zinc to prevent corrosion and extend their lifespan. The "others" category includes various types of brackets that do not fit into the previous three categories and may offer unique features or designs.</p></p>
