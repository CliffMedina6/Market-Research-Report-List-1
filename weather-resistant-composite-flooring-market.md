<p><p>The weather-resistant composite flooring market comprises two types - three layer and multilayer flooring. Three layer flooring consists of a core layer, a decorative layer, and a wear layer. It offers durability and stability, making it suitable for heavy foot traffic areas. Multilayer flooring, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers including a rigid core layer, decorative layer, and wear layer. It provides enhanced dimensional stability and can be installed directly over existing floors. Both types of flooring are designed to withstand moisture and fluctuations in temperature, making them ideal for outdoor and high-humidity environments.</p></p>
